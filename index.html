Research identity

From raw signals to reliable intelligence.

My research interests sit at the intersection of machine learning, speech brain-computer interfaces, audio understanding, and uncertainty-aware systems. I am especially drawn to problems where data is imperfect but the downstream decision still has to be useful, interpretable, and robust.

Recent work includes robust bird species identification from rainforest soundscapes, speech BCI baselines, EEG feature generation, and an uncertainty-aware data labeling tool with UTMIST and Themis AI.
